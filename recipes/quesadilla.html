Chicken Quesadillas Recipe

Recipe Description

This Chicken Quesadillas recipe is a simple yet flavorful way to prepare a cheesy and delicious Mexican-inspired dish. The quesadillas are filled with seasoned chicken, melted cheese, and vegetables of your choice. This recipe uses just a few basic ingredients and is perfect for a quick lunch or dinner.

Ingredients List

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 bell pepper, chopped
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack or cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream, salsa, and guacamole for serving

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Slice the chicken breasts in half horizontally to create 4 thin cutlets. In a small bowl, mix together the chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Rub the spice mixture onto both sides of the chicken cutlets.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken cutlets and cook for 5-6 minutes on each side, or until cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing it into strips.
  4. Add the chopped onion and bell pepper to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until softened. Remove the vegetables from the skillet and set them aside.
  5. Place a large flour tortilla on a baking sheet. Sprinkle 1/2 cup of shredded cheese on one half of the tortilla. Add some chicken strips and cooked vegetables on top of the cheese. Fold the other half of the tortilla over the filling and press down gently.
  6. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and filling ingredients.
  7. Bake the quesadillas in the preheated oven for 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the tortillas are lightly crispy.
  8. Serve the quesadillas hot, with sour cream, salsa, and guacamole on the side.
