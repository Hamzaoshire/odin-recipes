Steak Recipe

Recipe Description

This Pan-Seared Steak recipe is a simple yet flavorful way to prepare a juicy and tender steak. The steak is seared to perfection on the stovetop and finished off in the oven for an evenly cooked result. This recipe uses just a few basic ingredients and is perfect for a special occasion or a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients List

  • 2 boneless ribeye steaks, 1 1/2 inches thick
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season the ribeye steaks with salt and pepper on both sides, and let them sit at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat a large oven-safe skillet over high heat. Add 2 tbsp olive oil and let it heat up for 1 minute.
  4. Add the steaks to the skillet and sear for 3-4 minutes on each side, until browned and crispy.
  5. Add 2 tbsp unsalted butter, 3 crushed garlic cloves, and 2 sprigs of fresh thyme to the skillet. Spoon the melted butter over the steaks for 1-2 minutes until the garlic is fragrant and the thyme is crispy.
  6. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and cook for 6-8 minutes for medium-rare, or until the desired level of doneness is achieved.
  7. Remove the skillet from the oven and let the steaks rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.
